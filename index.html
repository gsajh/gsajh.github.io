About The Program

The Google Student Ambassador Program is a global initiative by Google that empowers student leaders to bridge the gap between academia and the tech industry. As the official GSA for Jamia Hamdard, my mission is to bring cutting-edge Google technologies, learning resources, and opportunities directly to our student community.


Our Goal

Empower Students by providing hands-on workshops, hackathons, and skill-building sessions.
Connect Innovators by creating a platform where students, developers, and industry experts collaborate.
Foster Growth by equipping students with the skills, tools, and network needed to excel in the tech ecosystem.


Opportunities for You

  • Participate in Google-hosted events and training sessions.
  • Access exclusive resources and certifications.
  • Network with like-minded innovators from across the globe.
  • Showcase your projects and get recognized at a higher level.
  • Contribute to open-source projects and gain real-world experience.
